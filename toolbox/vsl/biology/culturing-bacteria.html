Culturing Bacteria

Welcome Student Scientist!

This lesson will take a look into a biology lab technique called culturing bacteria. By the end of this lesson, our goal for you is to become more familiar with this lab skill, in hopes that you take this introductory knowledge with you in your future research endeavors.


This lesson includes:

  1. Background: Culturing Bacteria
  2. Simulation: Streak Plate Test
  3. Videos: Watch a series of videos covering bacteria culture techniques
  4. Quiz Yourself: Gauge your understanding of bacterial culturing techniques
  5. A Reach into Research: Antibiotic Resistance and Biofilms
