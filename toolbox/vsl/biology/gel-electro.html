Welcome Student Scientist!

This lesson will take a look into a biology lab technique called gel electrophoresis. By the end of this lesson, our goal for you is to become more familiar with this lab skill in hopes that you take this introductory knowledge with you in your future research endeavors.

This lesson includes:

  1. Background: Gel Electrophoresis
  2. Simulation: Gel Electrophoresis
  3. Video: Watch scientists perform gel electrophoresis in a lab
  4. Quiz Yourself: Gauge your understanding of the material
  5. A Reach into Research: How UC Berkeley scientists use gel electrophoresis in their research about CRISPR-Cas 9, bacterial immunity, and gene editing.
