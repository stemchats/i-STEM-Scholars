STEM Research Toolbox

The STEM Research Toolbox educates students about the basics of STEM research through publicly-accessible resources. The Toolbox walks you through how to understand scientific works, successfully perform labs, analyze the resulting datasets, and more!

Basics of Research

A webinar series that introduces students to the basic components of research.

Research Deep-Dive

A seminar that teaches students how to read and understand scientific papers.

Virtual Student Lab

A program that provides online lessons to improve laboratory skills and knowledge.
Data Analysis Workshop

A workshop series that teaches students how to analyze datasets for research purposes.

Finding Research Opportunities

A workshop series that will teach students how to find research opportunities.
